## Workflow

1. **Open User Management** (sidebar)
2. **Click "Add Employee"**
3. **Copy-paste** data from above
4. **Click "Create Account"**
5. **Repeat** for all 6 employees
6. **Go to Staff Directory**
7. **Click Edit** on each employee
8. **Add skills** from the list above
9. **Done!** ✅
